📊 About This Dashboard

🏗️ Leicester Flood Structures

990 flood defence structures and features from Leicester City Council. Shows walls, culverts, and other flood protection infrastructure.

📅 Historic Flood Events

127 documented flood events from 1946-2025. Red circles show locations. Click to see event date, location, and impact details.

🌊 EA Flood Zones (CCP1)

Environment Agency Flood Zones including climate change projections.
Zone 2: Medium risk (blue)
Zone 3: High risk (dark blue)
Zone 3a: +Climate change (red)

Data Sources

  • • Leicester City: Flood Risk Register (990 structures)
  • • Environment Agency: Historic Flood Map (127 events)
  • • Environment Agency: Flood Zones for Planning + CCP1

How to Use

  • 1. Click layer buttons to toggle data visibility
  • 2. Click on map features to view detailed information
  • 3. Use zoom controls to focus on specific areas
  • 4. Scroll to pan across the map
